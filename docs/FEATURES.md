# Features

This document outlines the key features of the **Unified Retailer Trust Scoring System (URTSS)**, designed to enhance consumer trust and decision-making in online marketplaces through AI-driven insights and privacy-first execution.

---

## 🟢 Core Features (MVP)

### 1. AI-Powered Unified Retailer Trust Score (0–100)

- Generates a single, consolidated trust score for each retailer
- Combines multiple behavioral and performance indicators into one metric
- Enables quick and confident decision-making for consumers

---

### 2. Explainable Trust Score Breakdown

- Provides transparent insights behind the trust score
- Includes:
  - Delivery Reliability Score
  - Review Authenticity Score
  - Customer Support Score
  - Refund & Return Fairness Score
- Eliminates black-box scoring and builds user trust

---

### 3. AI-Based Review Sentiment & Authenticity Analysis

- Uses NLP models to analyze review tone, intent, and context
- Identifies genuine, biased, or fake reviews
- Improves the accuracy and reliability of trust signals

---

### 4. Fake Review & Rating Manipulation Detection

- Detects abnormal rating spikes and suspicious review patterns
- Flags retailers engaging in potential rating manipulation
- Protects consumers from misleading seller behavior

---

### 5. Seller Comparison View

- Allows side-by-side comparison of multiple sellers offering the same product
- Displays trust scores and key trust indicators together
- Helps users select the safest and most reliable seller

---

### 6. Real-Time Trust Badge

- Displays a visual trust indicator next to seller listings
- Color-coded trust levels:
  - High Trust
  - Medium Trust
  - Low Trust
- Enables instant trust assessment during browsing

---

### 7. Local AI Execution (Privacy-First)

- AI models run directly on the user’s device
- No raw reviews or personal data sent to the cloud
- Ensures data privacy, reduced latency, and improved performance

---

### 8. Consumer Risk Alerts

- Proactively warns users before purchasing from risky sellers
- Alerts include:
  - High return rates
  - Low delivery reliability
  - Suspicious review activity
- Supports safer purchasing decisions

---

## 🟡 Advanced Features (Optional Enhancements)

### 9. Dynamic Trust Score Updating

- Trust scores update as new reviews or seller actions occur
- Reflects recent seller behavior in near real time

---

### 10. Context-Aware Trust Scoring

- Trust scores vary based on product category
- Example: Electronics vs fashion reliability differences

---

### 11. Seller Trust History Timeline

- Visual representation of trust score changes over time
- Helps users evaluate long-term seller consistency

---

## 🔵 Consumer Experience Enhancements

### 12. Smart Seller Recommendations

- Recommends sellers based on trust score and price
- Suggests safer alternatives when risk is detected

---

### 13. Trust-Based Search & Filters

- Filters sellers by minimum trust score
- Sorts results by most reliable sellers first

---

## 🔴 Future & Innovation Features

### 14. Product Image Authenticity Analysis

- Detects misleading or duplicate product images
- Flags potential counterfeit listings

---

### 15. Cross-Marketplace Trust Identity

- Maintains seller trust reputation across multiple platforms
- Enables unified trust evaluation

---

### 16. Voice-Based Trust Query

- Allows users to ask questions like  
  “Is this seller reliable?”
- AI responds with trust insights in natural language
